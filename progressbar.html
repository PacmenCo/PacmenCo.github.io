What is the Progress Bar?

The Progress Bar is a core progression mechanic that automatically fills up as you play. It represents your combat progress through zones and provides regular rewards including pixels, XP, and has a chance to trigger modest bonus effects.

Automatic Rewards: Every time the bar fills to 100%, you gain pixels, XP, and have a chance to trigger up to 6 different bonus perks!

How the Progress Bar Fills

The bar fills automatically over time based on your progress speed. The fill rate is calculated every 0.05 seconds using:

Progress Rate = 0.6 √ó Speed Factor √ó Delta Time / 0.05

Active vs Idle Mode

Mode Speed Factor Description
Active 1.0 (100%) When you're actively playing (keyboard/mouse input)
Idle 0.30 (30%) After 10 seconds of no input

The game automatically detects player activity and adjusts the progress speed. After 10 seconds of no input, the bar fills at 30% speed. Any keyboard or mouse movement resets the idle timer.

Rewards When the Bar Fills

Every time the progress bar reaches 100%, you receive:

Pixels

Base = (0.75 + 1.55 √ó 0.25) √ó floor(level^0.875) √ó Pixel Modifier
Final pixels = Random(0.02-0.45) √ó Base + Random(0-4)

Higher levels grant more pixels per progress completion.

Experience Points

XP Gain = Base XP √ó Random(0.2, 0.6)
Where base XP is the XP of the last defeated enemy (if that enemy gives more than the current Base XP) Note: Base XP multiplier decreases by 13.5% each completion (retains 86.5%)

Perk Triggers

Each time the bar fills, all 6 perks have a chance to activate based on their individual trigger chances. You can upgrade each perk to increase its activation chance!

üí° Strategy Tips

  • Prioritize Double Progress: This perk effectively doubles your rewards since it fills the bar again instantly. Max this first!
