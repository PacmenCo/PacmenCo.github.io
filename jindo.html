How to Activate Alpha Rush

Alpha Rush can be activated when specific combat conditions are met. Understanding these triggers is key to maximizing Jindo's effectiveness.

Activation Prerequisites

  • Enemy HP Below 35%: Primary trigger - ability becomes available when enemy drops below 35% HP
  • Enemy Critical Hit on You: Defensive trigger - activates when an enemy lands a critical hit on you
  • Minimum 3 Stamina: Jindo must have at least 3 stamina available
  • Jindo Must Be Awake: Cannot activate while sleeping
  • Enemy Must Be Alive: Cannot activate if enemy is already defeated

Combat Strategy

Timing Your Burst

Alpha Rush is most effective as a finishing move. Wait for enemies to drop below 35% HP, then unleash the burst to secure kills quickly. The massive damage and crit boost makes it devastating against weakened foes.

Stamina Management

Alpha Rush costs 3 stamina per attack and only lasts 1 attack. Ensure Jindo has sufficient stamina before crucial fights. Consider letting Jindo rest before boss encounters to maximize burst potential.

Defensive Trigger Usage

The critical hit activation trigger allows Jindo to respond to enemy burst damage with a powerful counter-attack. This makes Jindo surprisingly effective against crit-heavy enemies.

Auto-Activation Investment

With low SPECIAL (8-15), manual activation is more reliable. At 20+ SPECIAL, auto-activation becomes viable at ~10-17% chance. For pure damage, invest in STR/LUCK instead unless you value the convenience.
