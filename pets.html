What Are Pets?

Pets are passive followers in Idle Adventure that provide valuable stat bonuses to strengthen your character. Unlike Loyal Companions, which actively fight alongside you with complex abilities, pets offer two distinct types of bonuses: permanent bonuses and equipped bonuses.

Key Feature: With 93+ unique pets to collect, each pet provides one permanent stat bonus that's always active when owned, plus additional stats when equipped. Only one pet can be equipped at a time, but all owned pets contribute their permanent bonuses simultaneously.

Dual Bonus System

  • Permanent Bonuses: Always active from all owned pets, regardless of which is equipped
  • Equipped Bonuses: Full stat bonuses and combat damage only available from the currently selected pet
  • Stackable Benefits: Permanent bonuses from all pets stack together for powerful cumulative bonuses
  • Strategic Selection: Choose which pet to equip based on your current needs and build

Pet Leveling System

Pets gain experience primarily by being equipped while you defeat enemies. Your equipped pet earns +1 XP from each enemy defeated, but only from enemies that meet the pet's enemy level requirement.

Important: Each pet has a fixed enemy level requirement that never changes. Early-game pets (like Sunflower) can gain XP from weak enemies, while late-game pets (like Duck or Muscle Bob) require much stronger enemies to earn XP. Each qualifying enemy defeated grants exactly +1 XP.

How Pets Gain Experience

  • Primary Method - Combat: Equip your pet and defeat enemies that meet its enemy level requirement (+1 XP per enemy)
  • Fixed Requirement: Each pet's enemy level requirement is fixed - it doesn't change as the pet gains XP levels (0-5)
  • Secondary Method - Pet Food: Feed pets directly using pet food items (250 XP per food)
Pro Tip: Save your pet food for high-level pets! Since it's not possible to idle grind the highest level pets in the game, pet food becomes invaluable for leveling them. Use combat to level low-level pets naturally, and reserve pet food for your high-level pets where active grinding becomes impractical.

Level Progression

Pets have a base maximum of Level 3, which can be increased by +2 levels through ascension perks to reach Level 5. Each level increases the effectiveness of their stat bonuses:

Level 3 10,500 - 22,999 XP 1.0x multiplier

Ascension Benefits: The base maximum pet level is 3, but can be increased by +2 levels through ascension perks to reach Level 5, allowing you to further boost your pet collection's power.

Leveling Tips

  • Equip and Fight: Keep your pet equipped during combat to earn +1 XP per enemy defeated
  • Enemy Level Requirements: Each pet has a fixed enemy level requirement - early-game pets like Sunflower gain XP from weak enemies, while late-game pets require much stronger enemies
  • Match Pet to Area: Fight in areas appropriate for that specific pet's enemy level requirement (this never changes as the pet gains XP)
  • Strategic Pet Food Use: Each pet food grants 250 XP - save it for high-level pets
  • Level Cap: Base maximum is Level 3 (+2 from ascension perks to reach Level 5)
  • XP Scaling: Level 5 requires 43,000+ total XP from equal-or-higher level enemy kills!

Permanent Bonuses (Always Active)

The most powerful feature of the pet system is that every pet you own provides a permanent bonus bonus that contributes to your character's power regardless of which pet is equipped.

Critical Understanding: Permanent bonuses from ALL owned pets stack together. As you expand your pet collection, these cumulative bonuses significantly strengthen your character, making pet collection a long-term progression goal.

How Permanent Bonuses Work

  • Level 1 Required: Permanent bonuses only become active when the pet reaches Level 1 or higher
  • Level 0 Pets Give Nothing: Newly unlocked pets at Level 0 provide NO permanent bonus until leveled
  • No Equipment Required: You receive the bonus even if another pet is equipped
  • Scales with Level: Permanent bonuses benefit from the pet's level multiplier (Level 0: 0x, Level 1: 0.2x, up to 1.5x at Level 5)
  • One Per Pet: Each pet provides exactly one type of permanent bonus
  • Cumulative Power: Collecting more pets creates increasingly powerful synergies
Quick Win: Level new pets to Level 1 as soon as possible! It only takes 500 XP to reach Level 1, and this unlocks 20% of the pet's permanent bonus potential. This is a fast way to start benefiting from newly unlocked pets.

Strategic Value

Because permanent bonuses accumulate across your entire collection, every pet unlock matters. Even pets you never plan to equip provide lasting value through their permanent bonuses. This makes pet collection a rewarding long-term goal that continuously strengthens your character.

Pro Tip: Prioritize leveling pets with permanent bonuses that complement your build. A pet with +Crit Chance permanent bonus becomes more valuable to crit-focused builds when leveled to 1.5x multiplier at Level 5.

Equipped Bonuses & Combat (Active Pet Only)

While permanent bonuses provide passive benefits from all pets, equipped bonuses are only active from your currently selected pet. You can only have one pet equipped at a time, making pet selection a strategic choice.

Equipped Pet Benefits

  • Full Stat Bonuses: All of the equipped pet's stats apply to your character
  • Passive Benefits: Most pets provide only stat bonuses when equipped
  • Combat Pets: Some pets can attack enemies, but they are usually not very strong attackers

Pet Combat System

Most pets do not attack - they simply provide stat bonuses when equipped. Some pets have the ability to attack enemies, but their damage contribution is typically minimal compared to your own combat effectiveness.

Selecting Your Active Pet

Choose your equipped pet based on:

  • Current Build: Match pet stats to your character's strengths
  • Enemy Type: Use Boss Damage pets for boss encounters
  • Farming Goals: Equip Loot Chance or XP Modifier pets when grinding
  • Survival Needs: Switch to HP or Armor pets for difficult content
Remember: While you can only equip one pet at a time, changing your equipped pet is free and instant. Experiment with different pets to find what works best for each situation.

How to Unlock Pets

There are plenty of ways to unlock pets in Idle Adventure, each tied to different aspects of gameplay. Common unlock methods include:

  • Kill Count: Defeat specific numbers of certain enemy types
  • Special Area High Scores: Achieve high scores in Special Areas
  • Mysteries: Some pets require you to solve a mystery to unlock them
  • Progression Milestones: Reach certain levels, complete quests, or collect other pets
Tip: Check the pet collection menu to see specific unlock requirements for each pet and track your progress.

Optimization Strategies

Maximize the effectiveness of your pet collection with these strategic approaches:

Early Game Focus

  • Collect Everything: Every pet adds permanent bonuses, so unlock pets through natural gameplay progression
  • Level Strategically: Focus pet food on pets with permanent bonuses matching your build
  • Balanced Equipped Pet: Choose pets with well-rounded stats for general content

Mid Game Optimization

  • Targeted Unlocks: Actively pursue pets with high-value permanent bonuses (Crit Chance, Damage%, XP Modifier)
  • Situational Swapping: Keep multiple leveled pets for different scenarios (bosses, farming, survival)
  • Ascension Planning: Invest in ascension skills that increase maximum pet level

Late Game Mastery

  • Complete the Collection: Pursue high-requirement pets for their powerful permanent bonuses
  • Max Level Priority Pets: Get key pets to maximum level for 1.5x permanent bonus multiplier
  • Build-Specific Equipped Pets: Maintain a stable of specialized pets for every situation
Pro Tip: Match your equipped pet to your activity. Use XP Modifier pets when grinding, Boss Damage pets for bosses, and Loot Chance pets when farming for item type drops.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are pets in Idle Adventure?
Pets are passive followers in Idle Adventure that provide permanent bonus bonuses and equipped bonuses. With 93+ unique pets to collect, each pet grants one permanent bonus modifier that's always active when owned, plus additional stats and automated damage when equipped. Only one pet can be equipped at a time.
How do I level up my pets?
The primary way to level pets is by keeping them equipped while defeating enemies that meet the pet's enemy level requirement. Each pet has a fixed enemy level requirement - early-game pets like Sunflower can gain XP from weak enemies, while late-game pets require much stronger enemies. Pets gain +1 XP per qualifying enemy defeated. You can also use pet food (250 XP per food) as a secondary method, especially for late-game pets. Pets have a base maximum of Level 3 (+2 from ascension perks to reach Level 5). Permanent bonuses scale with level: Level 0 = 0x (no permanent bonus), Level 1 = 0.2x, Level 2 = 0.5x, Level 3 = 1x, Level 4 = 1.25x, Level 5 = 1.5x multiplier.
What's the difference between permanent bonuses and equipped bonuses?
Permanent bonuses are active from all owned pets at Level 1 or higher and stack together, providing passive bonuses regardless of which pet is equipped. Level 0 pets give no permanent bonus until leveled. Equipped stats only apply when a pet is selected as your active pet. You can only have one pet equipped at a time.
How do I unlock new pets?
Pets unlock through various methods: killing specific enemy types, reaching fishing levels, completing quest milestones, achieving dungeon scores, collecting items, or owning a certain number of pets. Each pet has unique unlock requirements displayed in the pet collection menu.
What pet stats are available?
Pets can provide 22 different stat types: HP, HP%, Mana, Mana Regen, HP Regen, HP Regen%, Damage, Damage%, Speed%, Loot Chance, XP Modifier, Armor, Armor%, Resistance, Evasion, Accuracy, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Reflect, Spawn Rate, Open Wounds, and Boss Damage.
Can I have multiple pets active at once?
You can only have ONE pet equipped at a time for equipped bonuses and combat damage. However, ALL owned pets contribute their permanent bonus bonuses simultaneously, meaning your entire pet collection passively strengthens your character.
Should I focus on leveling one pet or many pets?
Prioritize leveling pets with permanent bonuses that benefit your build, as these bonuses apply regardless of which pet is equipped. Once key permanent bonuses are maxed, level the pets you frequently equip. Remember that permanent bonuses from all pets stack, so broad collection growth provides cumulative benefits.
Do pets work with Loyal Companions?
Yes! Pets and Loyal Companions are separate systems that work together. You can have both one equipped pet and one active companion simultaneously. Pet bonuses (both permanent and equipped) apply to your character and don't interfere with companion mechanics.
