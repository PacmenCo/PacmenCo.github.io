Sticky Ruin!

A control-focused ability that guarantees a critical hit while massively slowing enemies and extending Globbie's attack range. Perfect for kiting dangerous enemies and controlling the battlefield.

  • Guaranteed Critical Hit: Next attack has +150% crit chance (guaranteed crit) and +2.0 crit damage
  • Active Slow: 67-81% slow for 10 seconds (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Passive Slow: 10-25% slow on every hit while active (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Range Bonus: +3m range (+0.6m effective after scaling)
  • Cooldown: 18 seconds
  • Stamina Cost: 3 stamina to activate

Slow Mechanics

Sticky Ruin provides two types of slows that work together to control enemy movement:

Active Slow (On Special Activation)

This powerful slow applies when you activate Sticky Ruin and hit an enemy. It lasts 10 seconds and reduces enemy acceleration dramatically. The slow starts at 67% and asymptotically approaches 86% at very high SPECIAL values.

Passive Slow (Continuous)

Slow % = 10 + 20 √ó (1 - 30/(SPECIAL + 30))

While Sticky Ruin is active, every hit applies a passive slow that stacks with the active slow. This scales from 10% to ~30% with high SPECIAL investment, providing continuous control over enemy positioning.

Guaranteed Critical Hit

The next attack after activating Sticky Ruin gains +150% crit chance (guaranteeing a critical hit) and +2.0 crit damage multiplier. This is perfect for burst damage against slowed enemies or finishing off dangerous targets.

Extended Range

When Sticky Ruin is active and you have sufficient stamina, Globbie gains +3m range (which translates to +0.6m effective range after division by 5). This range bonus allows you to kite enemies safely while dealing damage from a distance. Base range: 0.24m ‚Üí Active range: 0.84m

Slow Note: The slow effect reduces enemy acceleration, not just speed. This means slowed enemies take longer to reach their maximum movement speed, making it easier to maintain distance and control positioning.

‚ö° Activation Prerequisites

To use Sticky Ruin effectively:

  • Manual Activation Required: Must press the Sticky Ruin button (no auto-activation)
  • Sufficient Stamina: Must have at least 3 stamina
  • Companion Awake: Companion must not be sleeping
  • Not on Cooldown: 18-second cooldown must be complete

Effect Duration

Once activated, Sticky Ruin's effects apply on the next attack and remain active until you deactivate it or run out of stamina. The active slow lasts 10 seconds from the time it's applied, while the passive slow continues on every hit as long as the ability remains active.

Pro Tip: The 18-second cooldown is short compared to most other companions. You can activate Sticky Ruin frequently to maintain consistent crowd control, making Globbie excellent for zones with dangerous fast enemies.

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Strategy

When to Use Sticky Ruin

Activate Sticky Ruin when:

  • Facing fast enemies that close distance quickly
  • Fighting dangerous enemies you want to kite
  • Boss encounters where positioning matters
  • You need a guaranteed crit for burst damage
  • You have 3+ stamina and the 18s cooldown is ready

Kiting Strategy

The extended range (+0.6m effective) combined with 67-81% slow creates a powerful kiting setup. Activate Sticky Ruin, hit the enemy for the guaranteed crit and massive slow, then use the extended range to maintain distance while dealing damage. The 10-second slow duration gives plenty of time to reposition.

Cooldown Management

With only an 18-second cooldown, Sticky Ruin has one of the shortest cooldowns among companions. This means you can activate it frequently. Don't save it‚Äîuse it on cooldown for tough enemies or whenever you need crowd control. The passive slow remains active between cooldowns if you keep the ability enabled.

Stamina Conservation

Sticky Ruin costs 3 stamina, and each attack costs 1 stamina. With base 4 stamina, you can activate the ability and get 1 attack before needing to rest. Higher CON or SPECIAL increases max stamina, allowing multiple attacks per activation. Plan your stamina usage to maximize uptime.

Burst Damage Combo

The guaranteed crit provides excellent burst potential:

  • Activate Sticky Ruin when facing a priority target
  • Next attack: Guaranteed crit with +2.0 crit damage and full STR scaling
  • Enemy is now massively slowed for 10 seconds
  • Continue attacking with extended range and passive slow
  • Repeat every 18 seconds
