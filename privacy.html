Privacy Policy

Idle Adventure by Zymosis (PacmenCo)

Information Collected

Idle Adventure collects minimal data necessary for gameplay functionality:

  • Account information (username, login credentials)
  • Game progress and save data
  • Guild membership and chat messages

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Enable multiplayer features (guilds, guild bosses)
  • Provide guild chat functionality

Third-Party Services

Idle Adventure uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – for serving advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services – for authentication and app distribution.

Data Storage & Security

Your game data is stored on a secure server to enable cloud saving and multiplayer features.

Passwords are securely hashed before being stored — they are never saved in plain text, and not even the developer is able to see or retrieve them. All authentication data is encrypted before transmission, preventing interception through man-in-the-middle attacks.

Children's Privacy

Idle Adventure is not directed at children under the age of 13. Personal information from children under 13 is not knowingly collected.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out on Discord.
