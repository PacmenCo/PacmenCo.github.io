Privacy Policy

Idle Adventure by Zymosis (PacmenCo)

Information Collected

Idle Adventure collects minimal data necessary for gameplay functionality:

  • Account information (username, login credentials)
  • Game progress and save data
  • Guild membership and chat messages

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Enable multiplayer features (guilds, guild bosses)
  • Provide guild chat functionality

In-App Purchases

Idle Adventure offers optional in-app purchases. All transactions are processed securely through Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We do not collect or store any payment information — all billing is handled entirely by the respective platform.

Third-Party Services

Idle Adventure uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – for serving advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers. On iOS, you will be prompted via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before any tracking occurs. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services – for authentication and app distribution on Android.
  • Apple App Store – for app distribution and in-app purchases on iOS. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Security

Your game data is stored on a secure server to enable cloud saving and multiplayer features.

Passwords are securely hashed before being stored — they are never saved in plain text, and not even the developer is able to see or retrieve them. All authentication data is encrypted before transmission, preventing interception through man-in-the-middle attacks.

Children's Privacy

Idle Adventure is not directed at children under the age of 13. Personal information from children under 13 is not knowingly collected.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, all personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at pacmenco@gmail.com or reach out on Discord.
