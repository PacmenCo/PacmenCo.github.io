Vital Bloom!

A unique healing ability with dual mechanics: passive healing on every hit, and an active 3-attack sequence that drains HP twice before delivering a powerful heal on the third hit.

  • Passive: Every hit heals you for 1-3 HP (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Active (Manual): Triggers a 3-attack drain/heal sequence
  • Attacks 1-2: Drain ~4-10% of your max HP per attack
  • Attack 3: Heal ~16-29% of your max HP
  • Stamina Cost: 3 stamina to activate sequence

Dual Healing System

Mystikat provides two independent healing sources. The passive healing triggers on every successful hit regardless of whether Vital Bloom is active, providing consistent sustain. The active ability must be manually triggered and requires landing 3 consecutive hits to complete the sequence.

‚ö° Activation Prerequisites

Mystikat's passive healing works automatically, but Vital Bloom's active sequence requires:

  • Manual Activation: Must press the Vital Bloom button (no auto-activation)
  • Sufficient Stamina: Must have at least 3 stamina
  • Companion Awake: Companion must not be sleeping
  • Three Consecutive Hits: Must land 3 hits to complete the sequence
Important: If you miss an attack or the enemy dies mid-sequence, the drain/heal cycle is interrupted and resets. The HP drained will not be recovered unless you complete the full 3-attack sequence.

Sequence Management

The Vital Bloom sequence tracks charges (0, 1, 2). After the 3rd attack heals you, the sequence automatically deactivates and resets to 0 charges. You must manually reactivate it to start another drain/heal cycle. There is no cooldown‚Äîyou can immediately reactivate after completing a sequence if you have 3 stamina available.

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Strategy

Passive Healing Management

The passive healing is always active and requires no management. With higher DEX, you'll heal more frequently due to faster attacks. This passive sustain alone can keep you alive through many zones without ever using Vital Bloom.

When to Use Vital Bloom

Activate Vital Bloom when:

  • Your HP is below 70% and you need a burst heal
  • Fighting tough enemies or bosses where the net +9.5 HP gain matters
  • You have 3+ stamina available and won't need it for other companions
  • The enemy will survive all 3 hits (don't waste it on low-HP enemies)

Risk/Reward Calculation

The drain phase takes approximately 20% of your max HP over 2 attacks before healing ~29% back on the 3rd. If you're at low HP, using Vital Bloom is risky‚Äîthe drain could kill you before the heal lands. Always ensure you can survive 2 drains before activating.

Stamina Conservation

With 9 base stamina and 3 stamina cost per sequence, you can perform 3 full Vital Bloom cycles before needing rest. Higher CON builds can maintain near-permanent uptime. Don't spam it‚Äîsave stamina for when you actually need healing.

Boss Encounters

Mystikat excels in extended boss fights. The passive healing keeps you alive while the active sequences provide burst healing to recover from damage spikes. Time your Vital Bloom activations to complete the sequence before major boss mechanics hit.
