✨ Talents

Permanent passive bonuses to customize your character's strength

What are Talents?

Talents are permanent passive bonuses that can be purchased using Talent Points. The talent system is organized as a skill tree with interconnected nodes, each providing various stat bonuses to improve your character's power in combat, survivability, and utility.

Key Features

  • Free respec - reset your talents at any time with no cost
  • Save and load talent builds for easy switching
  • Three talent trees: Offensive, Defensive, and Utility
  • Prerequisites system - unlock advanced talents by investing in earlier ones

Earning Talent Points

Talent Points are earned primarily by gaining ranks. Each time you rank up, you receive 1 talent point.

Talent Point Formula

Available Talent Points = Rank + Bought Points + Ascension Bonuses + Purchase Bonus - Spent Points

Talent Point Sources

  • Ranks: 1 talent point per rank (primary source)
  • Purchased Points: Can be bought separately from the shop
  • Ascension Skill - "MoreSkill": +3 points per level (max 3 levels = +9 total)
  • +5 points for purchasing the talent tree unlock
One-Time Bonus: Purchasing the talent tree unlock grants a permanent bonus of 5 extra talent points.

How to Gain Ranks

Ranks are earned by accumulating Rank XP from defeating enemies.

Base Rank XP = 0.7 × randomFactor × (enemyLevel^1.1) + 3
Final Rank XP = Base × Modifiers

Rank XP Modifiers include:

  • Difficulty modifier (Easy/Normal/Hard/Hardcore)
  • Pet XP bonuses
  • Talent XP bonuses (Learner talents: +2% per level)
  • XP buffs from consumables
  • Ascension modifiers (+3% per Normal, +4% per Hard, +5% per Hardcore)
  • Ascension Skill - "BattleBrainy" (+10% per level, max 50%)
Over-leveling Penalty: If your rank is 3+ levels above the enemy, XP is reduced by the formula: `31 / (playerRank - enemyLevel + 28)`

XP Required Per Rank

XP Needed = 0.9801 × (currentRank^3) + 200 × currentRank - 101

Talent Point Costs

Each talent has an increasing cost per level. The cost rises with each talent level you invest.

Cost Formula

Cost per Level = Base Cost + Current Talent Level

Cost Examples

For a talent with base cost 1:

  • Level 1: costs 1 SP (total: 1)
  • Level 2: costs 2 SP (total: 3)
  • Level 3: costs 3 SP (total: 6)
  • Level 4: costs 4 SP (total: 10)

Talents have different base costs (1, 2, or 3 SP) and different maximum levels (1-4 levels depending on the talent).

Talent Trees

Talents are organized into three trees, each focusing on a different aspect of character power.

Offensive Tree

Boosts your damage output, attack speed, accuracy, and critical strikes.

  • Attack Power: Flat damage bonus (various levels)
  • Strength: +1.5% damage per level (max 4)
  • Speed: +2% attack speed per level (max 4)
  • Crit Chance: +1% crit chance per level (max 3)
  • Crit Damage: +7.5% crit damage per level (max 3)
  • Accuracy: +1 accuracy per level (max 3)

Defensive Tree

Improves your survivability through HP, armor, resistances, and damage mitigation.

  • Health: +20 flat HP per level (max 3)
  • Endurance: +4% HP per level (max 3)
  • Armor: +4 flat armor per level (max 3)
  • Hardiness: +2.5% armor per level (max 4)
  • Resistance: +3% damage reduction per level (max 3)
  • Resilience: +4% crit damage reduction per level (max 3)
  • Evasion: +1 evasion per level (max 3)

Utility Tree

Enhances XP gain, loot drops, mana pool, and utility mechanics.

  • Learner: +2% XP gain per level (max 3)
  • Luck: +3% loot frequency per level (max 3)
  • Mana: +10 flat mana per level (max 3)
  • Spirit: +12.5% mana regen per level (max 4)
  • Cure Poison: 35% chance to cure poison when healing per level (max 2)

Talent Effectiveness

All talent effects can be amplified through ascension skills.

Actual Effect = Base Effect × (1 + SkillQuality Ascension Skill)

The SkillQuality ascension skill provides +3% effectiveness per level (max 5 levels = +15% improvement to all talents).

Example

A Strength talent normally gives +1.5% damage. With SkillQuality at level 5:

Actual Damage = 1.5% × (1 + 0.15) = 1.5% × 1.15 = 1.725% damage per level

Talent Respec & Builds

Free Respec

Talents can be reset at any time for FREE. There is no cost or penalty for resetting your talents.

  • Click the "Reset Talents" button
  • All spent talent points are refunded instantly
  • No cooldown or restrictions

Talent Builds

Save your favorite talent configurations for easy switching between builds.

  • Save builds locally with custom names
  • Load saved builds instantly
  • If a build requires more points than you have, it automatically resets
  • Perfect for switching between PvE, PvP, or boss-specific builds
