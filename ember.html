PyroMantic

A powerful burn-based ability that stacks damage over time before detonating all stacks for massive damage.

  • Passive: Every hit applies an Ember stack to enemies
  • Active (35s cooldown): Consumes all Ember stacks, dealing massive damage per stack
  • Guaranteed Critical: 100% crit chance when consuming stacks
  • Stamina Cost: 2 stamina to activate special, 1 per normal attack

Burn Mechanics

Ember stacks are independent DoT (damage over time) effects that can coexist on enemies. Each stack deals consistent damage over 8 seconds. When you activate PyroMantic, all stacks are consumed simultaneously for a devastating burst of guaranteed critical damage.

‚ö° Activation Prerequisites

To activate PyroMantic, the following conditions must be met:

  • Not on Cooldown: 35 second cooldown must be complete
  • Sufficient Stamina: Must have at least 2 stamina
  • Companion Awake: Companion must not be sleeping
  • Enemy Exists: A valid enemy must be in combat
  • Enemy is Burned: Target MUST have at least one active Ember stack
Critical Requirement: You cannot activate PyroMantic unless the enemy has active burn stacks. This means you need to land hits first to apply burns before you can detonate them.

Stack Management

When you reach max stacks and apply a new burn, the soonest-to-expire burn is immediately removed and replaced. This means maintaining maximum stacks requires consistent attacks, but also means you won't waste damage by over-stacking.

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Strategy

Burn Build-Up Phase

Focus on applying maximum burn stacks before activating PyroMantic. With higher DEX, this happens naturally, but with lower attack speed you may need to wait for several attacks to land before the special is worth activating.

Optimal Detonation Timing

Activate PyroMantic when you have maximum stacks and the cooldown is available. Don't wait for stacks to expire‚Äîthe 35-second cooldown means you want to use it as soon as conditions are met. With max stacks, a single PyroMantic activation deals 170% of the total burn damage all stacks would have dealt over time, plus guaranteed critical multiplier.

Cooldown Management

After using PyroMantic, immediately start re-applying burns. The 35-second cooldown means you'll typically build 3-6+ full stack cycles between special uses. Don't hold the special‚Äîuse it on cooldown for maximum damage throughput.

Stamina Conservation

Each attack costs 1 stamina, and PyroMantic costs 2. With base 3 stamina + SPECIAL/7 + CON/5, you can perform several attack cycles before needing to rest. Higher CON builds can maintain near-permanent uptime.

Boss Encounters

Ember excels against bosses due to sustained burn damage. Build up full stacks, detonate with PyroMantic, then rebuild. The guaranteed crit on detonation makes this extremely effective against high-HP targets.
