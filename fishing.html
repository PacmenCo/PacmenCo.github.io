Skill-based fishing with levels, combos, and upgradeable rods

Overview

Fishing is a comprehensive mini game with two phases: throwing and reeling. Master both mechanics to catch valuable fish, treasure, and level up your fishing skill. The system features seven upgradeable rods, combo multipliers, and three types of catches.

Where to Fish: Find fishing spots in specific zones throughout your adventure. Look for barrels near water!

How to Fish

Throwing Phase

A marker moves across the screen. Release when the marker is in the center for a Perfect throw! There are 7 accuracy levels:

  • Perfect: Best accuracy - lowest patience cost (2), highest treasure chance
  • Awesome: Excellent - low patience cost (5), good treasure chance
  • Great: Good - moderate patience cost (7)
  • Good: Average - higher patience cost (9)
  • Ok: Below average - high patience cost (10)
  • Bad: Poor - very high patience cost (11)
  • Ouch: Worst - maximum patience cost (12)

Reeling Phase

Keep the fish within the accuracy box by controlling your reel. The fish moves randomly, and you must maintain contact with it. A Perfect Reel (keeping the fish in the box the entire time) grants a 1.5√ó experience bonus!

Patience System

Patience is your fishing resource that depletes with each throw. Run out, and you can't fish anymore until it regenerates.

Maximum Patience = 40 + (2 √ó Fishing Level)

Better throws use less patience. Perfect throws only cost 2 patience, while Ouch throws cost 12. Patience regenerates passively over time (+15 per cycle).

Rod of Tranquility: This upgradeable rod has a chance to make throws cost zero patience!

Higher fishing levels increase your maximum patience and improve your chances of catching better fish.

Combo & Perfect Streak

Combo System

Build combos by landing Awesome or Perfect throws. Only Perfect throws increase the combo significantly (+3), while Awesome gives +1. Any other accuracy breaks your combo.

Combo Range Modifier Effect
0-9 +0 No bonus
10-24 +1 Better fish chances, slight treasure boost
25-49 +2 Improved fish quality, good treasure boost
50+ +3 Best fish chances, maximum treasure boost

Perfect Streak

Track consecutive Perfect throws separately. This streak only increases with Perfect accuracy and provides additional bonuses to treasure chances. Your best streak is saved!

Fish Types

There are three categories of catches, each with different purposes:

Regular Fish

Various fish species that provide pixels, experience, and fish weight. Better accuracy and higher combos increase your chances of catching rare fish species.

Junk

Boots, trash, and other unwanted catches. Easier to get with poor throws. Still provides some rewards but less valuable than fish.

Treasure

The most valuable catches! Treasure has a higher chance with Perfect and Awesome throws, especially with high combos:

  • Copper Treasure: Pixels (currency)
  • Silver Treasure: Voxels (crafting materials)
  • Crystal Treasure: Crystals (enchanting materials)
  • Voxel Treasure: Large voxel amounts
  • Gold Treasure: Gems and mixed rewards
  • Legendary Treasure: Special high-value rewards
Treasure Chances: Perfect throws have the best treasure chance, followed by Awesome. Combo multipliers significantly increase these odds!

Strategy Tips

  • Prioritize Perfect Throws: They use the least patience, build combos fastest, and have the best treasure chances
  • Upgrade Rod of Tranquility Early: Free throws save patience and let you fish much longer
  • Build High Combos: Combo modifiers dramatically improve treasure chances and fish quality
  • Golden Rod for Treasure: If you're hunting treasures, upgrade the Golden Rod for massive treasure boosts
  • Great Rod for Learning: Slower fish movement makes Perfect Reels much easier to achieve
  • Watch Your Patience: Don't waste throws with poor accuracy - it's better to wait for good positioning
