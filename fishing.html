Overview

Fishing is a mini game with two phases: throwing and reeling. Master both mechanics to catch valuable fish, treasure, and level up your fishing skill. Build combo points for better rewards and upgrade your fishing rods! multipliers.

Where to Fish: Fishing is unlocked early in the game, and you can find it in the 'Arrow Menu' at the top of the screen!

How to Fish

Throwing Phase

A marker moves across the screen. Release when the marker is on the red line for a Perfect throw! The better your accuracy the less patience you lose, and the higher chance of catching high tier fish or Treasure! There are 7 accuracy levels:

Accuracy levels:

  • Perfect: Best accuracy - lowest patience cost (2), highest treasure chance
  • Awesome: Excellent - low patience cost (5), good treasure chance
  • Great: Good - moderate patience cost (7)
  • Good: Average - higher patience cost (9)
  • Ok: Below average - high patience cost (10)
  • Bad: Poor - very high patience cost (11)
  • Ouch: Worst - maximum patience cost (12)

Reeling Phase

Keep the fish within the accuracy box by controlling your reel. The fish moves randomly, and you must maintain contact with it!

Patience System

Patience is your fishing resource that depletes with each throw. Run out, and you can't fish again before it regenerates.

Patience regenerates passively over time (+15 per cycle).

Rod of Tranquility: This upgradeable rod has a chance to make throws cost zero patience!

Combo & Perfect Streak

Combo System

Build combos by landing Awesome or Perfect throws. Only Perfect throws increase the combo significantly (+3), while Awesome gives +1. Any other accuracy breaks your combo.

Combo Range Modifier Effect
0-9 +0 No bonus
10-24 +1 Better fish chances, slight treasure boost
25-49 +2 Improved fish quality, good treasure boost
50+ +3 Best fish chances, maximum treasure boost

Perfect Streak

Track consecutive Perfect throws separately. This streak only increases with Perfect accuracy and provides additional bonuses to treasure chances. Your best streak is saved!

Fish Types

There are three categories of catches, each with different purposes:

Regular Fish

Various fish species that provide Pixels, Experience, and fish weight. Better accuracy and higher combos increase your chances of catching rare fish species.

Fish Weight Uses: Accumulated fish weight can be spent to purchase:
  • Experimental Mixture - A consumable with random beneficial or debuff effects
  • Pet Food - Feed to pets for instant XP boosts

Junk

Boots, trash, and other unwanted catches. Easier to get with poor throws. Still provides some rewards but less valuable than fish.

Treasure

The most valuable catches! Treasure has a higher chance with Perfect and Awesome throws, especially with high combos:

  • Copper Treasure: A good amount of Pixels
  • Silver Treasure: A large amount of Pixels
  • Gold Treasure: A huge amount of Pixels
  • Crystal Treasure: Many Crystals
  • Voxel Treasure: Large Voxel amounts
  • Small Chest: Can contain high-value rewards!
Treasure Chances: Perfect throws have the best treasure chance, followed by Awesome. Combo multipliers significantly increase these odds!

Strategy Tips

  • Perfect Throws: They use the least patience, build combos fastest, and have the best treasure chances
  • Upgrade Rod of Tranquility Early: Free throws save patience and let you fish much longer
  • Build High Combos: Combo modifiers dramatically improve treasure chances and fish quality
  • Golden Rod: If you're hunting treasures, upgrade the Golden Rod for massive treasure boosts
  • Great Rod: Slower fish movement makes Perfect Reels much easier to achieve
Fishing Challenges & Pets: Some tough fishing challenges reward exclusive pets! Check out the Pet Database to see which pets can be obtained through fishing.

FAQ

Why should I fish at all?
Fishing provides unique rewards you can't get elsewhere!
  • Exclusive Pets: Some pets are only obtainable through fishing challenges
  • Pet Food: Very helpful for leveling high-tier pets!
  • Experimental Mixture: Random beneficial buffs or debuffs for risky but potentially powerful effects
Plus, fishing provides Pixels, Experience, and treasure with Voxels, Crystals, and Gems!
What is the best fishing rod to upgrade first?
It depends on what you want from fishing! Each rod offers different benefits to suit various playstyles. Check the Fishing Rods table to see the detailed effects of each rod and decide which upgrade path matches your fishing goals.
How do combos work in fishing?
Combos build when you land Perfect (+3) or Awesome (+1) throws. Any other accuracy level breaks your combo. Combo modifiers increase at specific thresholds:
  • 10-24 combo: +1 modifier
  • 25-49 combo: +2 modifier
  • 50+ combo: +3 modifier
Higher combos dramatically improve treasure chances and fish quality. Focus on Perfect throws to build combos fastest!
Can I get pets from fishing?
Yes! Some tough fishing challenges reward exclusive pets. These are special achievements that require high fishing skill or specific accomplishments. Check the Pet Database to see which pets can be obtained through fishing challenges and their unlock requirements.
What's the difference between fish types?
There are three categories of catches:
  • Regular Fish: Provide Pixels, Experience, and fish weight. Better accuracy and combos increase chances of rare fish species.
  • Junk: Boots, trash, and unwanted items. Easier to catch with poor throws. Less valuable but still provides some rewards.
  • Treasure: Most valuable! Six rarity tiers (Copper, Silver, Crystal, Voxel, Gold, Legendary) providing Pixels, Voxels, Crystals, and Gems. Best obtained with Perfect throws and high combos.
How does the patience system work?
Patience is your fishing resource that depletes with each throw. Your maximum patience = 40 + (2 Ã— Fishing Level). Different throw accuracies cost different amounts:
  • Perfect: 2 patience (most efficient)
  • Awesome: 5 patience
  • Great: 7 patience
  • Good: 9 patience
  • Ok: 10 patience
  • Bad: 11 patience
  • Ouch: 12 patience (worst)
Patience regenerates passively at +15 per cycle. When you run out, you must wait for it to regenerate before fishing again. Rod of Tranquility can make throws cost zero patience!
Should I focus on Perfect throws or just fish quickly?
Always aim for Perfect throws! They provide multiple benefits:
  • Use the least patience (2 vs up to 12)
  • Build combos fastest (+3 per Perfect vs +1 for Awesome)
  • Highest treasure chances
  • Best fish quality
  • Most efficient XP gain per patience spent
Fishing quickly with poor accuracy wastes patience and yields worse rewards. Take your time to land Perfect throws consistently for maximum efficiency.
