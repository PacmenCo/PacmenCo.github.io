Classic arcade action with scaling difficulty and loot rewards

Overview

Crossy Cube is a tribute to the classic arcade game where you guide a frog through dangerous roads and rivers. Navigate through traffic, jump on logs to cross water, and collect valuable loot while the game gets progressively harder. How far can you make it?

Access: Crossy Cube is available from the mini games panel, which unlocks at level 5.

How to Play

Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD to move:

  • Up (W): Move forward
  • Down (S): Move backward
  • Left (A): Move left
  • Right (D): Move right

Movement is grid-based - you move one tile at a time with each key press. All movements are animated with a smooth jump.

Objective

Move forward as far as possible while avoiding hazards and collecting loot. Your score is based on how far forward you travel. Beat your high score and earn valuable resources!

Terrain Types

Grass (Safe)

Green tiles where you can safely stand and rest. No hazards here!

Roads (Dangerous)

Roads spawn moving obstacles (Evil Cubes) that travel left or right at varying speeds. Getting hit means instant death! Watch the traffic patterns and time your crossing carefully.

Water (Requires Logs)

You cannot swim! Water sections have floating logs you must jump onto. Stay on the logs to cross safely - falling in the water means death. Logs move left or right, so position yourself carefully.

Rails

Less common terrain type. Safe to cross like grass.

Boundary Deaths: Moving too far left or right (beyond the play area) will also result in death!

Scoring

Your score equals your furthest forward position. Moving backward doesn't decrease your score - only forward progress counts!

High Score System

Your best score is permanently saved. Each run displays both your current score and your all-time high score. Can you beat your record?

Death & Restart

Death occurs when you:

  • Get hit by an obstacle on a road
  • Fall into water (not on a log)
  • Move too far left or right beyond the boundary

When you die, a death animation plays, and after a brief delay, you're returned to the menu. You keep any loot collected during that run! Start a new game to try again.

Log Mechanics

Logs are critical for crossing water safely:

  • Boarding: Jump onto a log to ride it safely across water
  • Movement: While on a log, you move with it automatically
  • Log Sizes: Logs come in small (1 tile), medium (2 tiles), and large (3 tiles)
  • Direction: Logs move left or right at varying speeds
  • Repositioning: You can move left/right while on a log to adjust your position
Stay On the Log: Moving off a log while over water = instant death!

Strategy Tips

  • Watch Patterns: Obstacles move in predictable patterns - study them before crossing
  • Stay Centered: Don't move too far left or right - you'll hit the boundary
  • Time Your Jumps: Wait for gaps in traffic rather than trying to squeeze through
  • Log Planning: Look ahead at log positions before you commit to crossing water
  • Loot vs. Safety: Don't risk death for loot - staying alive for higher difficulty gives better rewards
  • Use Grass Tiles: Stop on grass to study the next few obstacles before proceeding
  • Push for High Scores: The further you go, the better the loot multipliers become
