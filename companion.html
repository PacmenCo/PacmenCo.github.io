Idle Adventure Companion Guide - All 9 Companions

Jindo - Burst Damage Specialist

Unlocks at level 5 for 500 gems. Special ability: Alpha Rush! Jindo has balanced STR and CON (both 11) for solid damage and survivability. Good base SPECIAL (8) provides decent Alpha Rush scaling. Low DEX (6) means slower attack speed. Moderate LUCK (9) offers fair crit potential. Best companion for burst damage, critical hits, and finishing vulnerable enemies. Scales with SPECIAL stat for increased damage and critical damage bonuses.

Ember - Burn Damage Specialist

Unlocks at level 15 for 2500 gems. Special ability: Flame Strike. Ember specializes in burn damage over time effects. High DEX for fast attacks that apply burn stacks. Excellent for prolonged fights where burn damage can stack and deal massive damage over time. Scales with SPECIAL stat for increased burn damage and duration.

Mystikat - Magic Support Companion

Unlocks at level 25 for 5000 gems. Special ability: Mystic Ward. Mystikat provides magical support with defensive buffs and mana regeneration. Balanced stats make it versatile for various playstyles. Excellent for magic-focused builds and players who need sustainability in long battles. Reduces incoming magic damage and provides team-wide buffs.

Hootini - Magical DPS Specialist

Unlocks at level 35 for 10000 gems. Special ability: Arcane Blast. Hootini focuses on raw magical damage output. Very high SPECIAL stat for maximum spell power. Low physical stats but compensates with devastating spell damage. Best companion for pure magic builds and boss encounters where burst magic damage is needed.

Globbie - Tank and Debuffer

Unlocks at level 45 for 25000 gems. Special ability: Goo Shield. Globbie has extremely high CON for maximum survivability. Provides defensive support and applies debuffs to enemies. Can absorb massive amounts of damage while weakening opponents. Essential for difficult content and progression walls where survivability is key.

Acornelius - Nature Support

Unlocks at level 55 for 50000 gems. Special ability: Forest Blessing. Acornelius provides nature-based buffs and healing over time. Excellent stat growth and versatile support abilities. Enhances resource regeneration and provides consistent healing. Great for balanced teams that need sustain without sacrificing damage.

Florence - Healing Support

Unlocks at level 65 for 100000 gems. Special ability: Divine Heal. Florence is the premier healing companion with powerful restoration abilities. Very high CON and healing power. Can keep your team alive through the toughest encounters. Essential for endgame content and ascension pushes where healing is critical.

Mechabird - Utility Support

Unlocks at level 75 for 250000 gems. Special ability: Overdrive Protocol. Mechabird provides unique utility buffs including attack speed, cooldown reduction, and resource efficiency. Balanced stats with focus on support. Excellent for optimizing combat efficiency and speeding up progression.

Snekles - Poison Specialist

Unlocks at level 85 for 500000 gems. Special ability: Venom Strike. Snekles specializes in deadly poison damage over time. Very high DEX for rapid poison application. Poison stacks scale exponentially making Snekles devastating against high HP enemies and bosses. Best companion for endgame content where maximum damage over time is needed.
