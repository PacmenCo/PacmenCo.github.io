What is Item Alteration?

Item Alteration is an advanced crafting system and gear modification mechanic that allows you to modify equipment properties using Alteration Orbs. Unlike enchanting, this item crafting system lets you reroll stats, add or remove affixes, upgrade rarity tiers, and improve item quality through strategic orb usage.

High Risk, High Reward: Some alteration types can break your item, reducing it to Broken rarity. Use with caution on valuable equipment!

Alteration vs Enchanting

Feature Enchanting Item Alteration
Success Rate 100% guaranteed Varies (some can break items)
Purpose Add fixed stat bonuses Reroll/modify item properties
Materials Pixels + Crystals Pixels + Crystals + Orbs
Complexity Simple and predictable Complex with many options
Cost Moderate Expensive

There are 11 different alteration orbs for item crafting and gear modification, each with unique effects and drop rates. These crafting materials are looted from enemies during combat and enable advanced equipment customization.

Orb of Divination Special Property: Normal crafted items usually have a maximum of 3 affixes (Legendary rarity) or 3-4 affixes (Artifact rarity). However, Orb of Divination can make items have up to 5 affixes! This makes it the only way to craft truly exceptional items that exceed natural limits. The orb adds 1-3 affixes to an item with exactly 2 affixes: 65% for 1 affix (3 total), 31% for 2 affixes (4 total), or 4% for 3 affixes (5 total).

Quality & Rarity Upgrades

Alteration orbs can improve item quality and upgrade rarity, but some operations carry significant risk. Understanding which orbs are safe versus risky is crucial for optimizing your gear without losing valuable equipment.

Orb of Fortune (Reroll Quality/Rarity)

  • 5% chance: Increase rarity by 1 tier
  • 50% chance: Item breaks (becomes Broken rarity)
  • 45% chance: Reroll item quality (stays same rarity)
  • Cannot be used on Artifact items
High Risk: Orb of Fortune has a 50% chance to break your item! Only use on items you're willing to lose.
Quality Reroll Benefit: The 45% quality reroll can significantly improve your item! Each rarity tier has a quality range that determines stat values. Rerolling quality gives you another chance to get higher values within your item's rarity bounds, potentially turning a low-roll Legendary into a high-roll one without changing its rarity.
Creating Artifacts: Orb of Fortune is the only way to upgrade a Legendary item to Artifact rarity through alteration. Radiant Orbs cannot be used on Legendary items.

Radiant Orb (Upgrade Rarity)

Upgrades item rarity by 1 tier with a chance to break based on current rarity:

Current Rarity Upgrades To Break Chance
Uncommon Rare 10%
Rare Epic 15%
Epic Legendary 20%
Legendary - Cannot be used
Artifact - Cannot be used

Quality Upgrades

  • Armor Plate: +1% to armor quality (no break chance)
  • Sharpening Stone: +1% to weapon quality (no break chance)
  • Can be upgraded 15+ times before hitting soft cap
  • Quality directly multiplies item stats

Soul Level System

Soul levels are a special item property that allows equipment to gain experience and level up over time. This system provides long-term power scaling for your best endgame gear, making properly soul-leveled items significantly stronger than standard equipment.

How Soul Levels Work

  • Items can have 5 soul levels maximum
  • Use Soulstones to add +1 max soul level per use
  • Only items with soul levels can gain experience and level up through regular gameplay
  • Items with souls gain experience as you defeat enemies
  • Leveling items will grant additional affixes and stat bonuses
Pro Tip: Reserve Soulstones for your best Legendary or Artifact items to maximize their long-term potential.

Strategy Tips

Alteration Strategy

  • Save rare Orbs (Orb of Divination, Radiant Orbs) for the best items
  • Never use Orb of Fortune on items you can't afford to lose
  • During hardcore ascensions, use orbs aggressively to progress faster

Pro Tips for Optimizing Gear with Alteration Orbs

  • How to farm Soulstones efficiently: Focus on specific farming areas rather than relying on the 0.11% drop rate from general combat
  • Best orbs for Legendary items: Use Orb of Divination on 2-affix Legendaries to create powerful 5-affix equipment
  • Min-maxing strategy: Use Armor Plate and Sharpening Stone repeatedly on your best gear for safe, guaranteed improvements
  • Rarity upgrade path: Use Radiant Orb on Epic items (20% break chance) rather than risking Orb of Fortune's 50% break rate
  • Affix management: Use Orb of Reversal to remove unwanted affixes before using Orb of Divination to fill slots with new random affixes
  • Resource allocation: Common orbs (Alteration, Transformation, Reversal) can be used freely, while extremely rare orbs should be reserved for Artifact-tier equipment

FAQ

What are Alteration Orbs in Idle Adventure?

Alteration Orbs are specialized crafting materials used to modify equipment properties in Idle Adventure. There are 11 different orb types that can reroll stats, add or remove affixes, upgrade rarity, and improve item quality. They drop from enemies during combat, with varying rarity levels.

Which Alteration Orbs can break items?

Orb of Fortune has a 50% chance to break items, making it the riskiest alteration. Radiant Orb has a 10-20% break chance depending on item rarity (10% for Uncommon, 15% for Rare, 20% for Epic). Safe orbs include Armor Plate, Sharpening Stone, and Soulstone, which have no break chance.

How do I get Soulstones in Idle Adventure?

Soulstones have an extremely low drop rate (0.11%) from regular combat, but they can be farmed in certain areas of the game for more reliable acquisition. Soulstones add +1 max soul level to equipment, allowing items to gain experience and level up over time.

What is the best Alteration Orb for endgame items?

Orb of Divination is the best for creating exceptional endgame gear. It can add 1-3 affixes to items with exactly 2 affixes, potentially creating 5-affix items which exceeds the normal 3-4 affix limit. This makes it the only way to craft truly min-maxed equipment that surpasses natural gear limitations.

How many orbs does it cost to alter Legendary and Artifact items?

Legendary items require 8 orbs per alteration. Artifact items require 20 orbs per alteration. Lower rarities cost less: Common/Poor/Broken need 1 orb, Uncommon needs 2 orbs, Rare needs 3 orbs, and Epic needs 4 orbs. Additionally, you'll need Pixels and Crystals.

Can I upgrade a Legendary item to Artifact rarity?

Yes, but only using Orb of Fortune. It has a 5% chance to upgrade rarity, a 50% chance to break the item, and a 45% chance to reroll quality without changing rarity. Radiant Orb cannot be used on Legendary items, making Orb of Fortune the only alteration method to reach Artifact rarity from Legendary.
