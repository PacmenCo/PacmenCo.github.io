Overview

Consumables provide temporary or permanent stat boosts to help you overcome difficult challenges. Elixirs grant powerful buffs with varying durations, potions (HP/Mana) offer instant recovery, while other consumables provide permanent bonuses.

Buff Stacking: Most elixirs stack with each other and can be refreshed by drinking another. Watch your active buffs in the UI!

Health Potion

Restores 65% of your maximum HP in two phases: 40% instantly (26% of max HP) and 60% over 6 seconds (39% of max HP). Scales with your health pool.

Effect: 65% Max HP (40% instant, 60% HoT) Cooldown: 0.5 seconds

Restores 175% of your maximum mana in two phases: 25% instantly (43.75% of max mana) and 75% over 32 seconds (131.25% of max mana). Scales with your mana pool.

Effect: 175% Max Mana (25% instant, 75% over time) Cooldown: 0.5 seconds

Reading this ancient scroll grants enhanced learning, boosting both level and rank experience gains. Perfect for accelerating your progression!

Effect: +50% XP Gain (1.5x) Duration: 30 minutes (1800s) Stackable: Yes (Refreshes)

Frequently Asked Questions

How do health and mana potions scale with stats?
Health potions restore 65% of your maximum HP (26% instantly and 39% over 6 seconds). Mana potions restore 175% of your maximum mana (43.75% instantly and 131.25% over 32 seconds). Both scale directly with your max HP and max mana, making both HP and Mana stats more valuable.
Can I stack multiple potions at once?
Yes, most consumables stack with each other. Elixirs (Strength, Endurance, Vigor, Agility, Luck) all stack together and last 630 seconds. Drinking the same elixir again refreshes the duration. However, Experimental Mixtures cannot stack with themselves to prevent negative effects.
What are the chances for Experimental Mixture effects?
Experimental Mixture has an equal 12.5% chance for each of 8 effects: 5 beneficial buffs (62.5% total chance, 20.5 min duration) and 3 debuffs (37.5% total chance, 3.2 min duration). Beneficial effects include Armor Boost, Mastery, Critical Power, Mana Efficiency, and Enhanced Healing. Debuffs include Weakened, Mana Drain, and Decreased Regeneration.
How do guild buffs work?
Guild buffs are powerful bonuses managed through your guild that affect both you and your companions. Currently available buffs include companion damage, companion defense, companion experience, and special buffs like increased damage against specific monster types. Player stat buffs are coming in a later update.
What happens when I use XP Scrolls or Drop Boosters while one is active?
Using another XP Scroll or Drop Booster while one is active will add 30 minutes to the remaining duration, not replace it. This allows you to stack duration and maintain these valuable +50% bonuses for extended periods.
