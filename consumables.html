üß™ Consumables & Buffs

Temporary and permanent enhancements to boost your power

Overview

Consumables provide temporary or permanent stat boosts to help you overcome difficult challenges. Potions grant powerful buffs with varying durations, while other consumables offer instant effects or permanent bonuses.

Buff Stacking: Most potions stack with each other and can be refreshed by drinking another. Watch your active buffs in the UI!

üß™ Standard Potions

These potions provide powerful temporary buffs with 630 second (10.5 minute) durations. All are stackable and can be refreshed.

Potion of Strength

Increases your damage output by 20%, making combat faster and more efficient.

Effect: +20% Damage Duration: 630 seconds Stackable: Yes

Potion of Endurance

Boosts your maximum health by 20%, providing better survivability in tough fights.

Effect: +20% Max HP Duration: 630 seconds Stackable: Yes

Potion of Vigor

Dramatically increases health regeneration by 35%, keeping you in the fight longer.

Effect: +35% Health Regen Duration: 630 seconds Stackable: Yes

Potion of Agility

Improves both accuracy and evasion, making you harder to hit while landing more attacks.

Effect: +5 Accuracy & Evasion Duration: 630 seconds Stackable: Yes

Potion of Luck

Grants the Lucky Strike buff, increasing critical hit chance.

Effect: Lucky Strike Rolls for critical hits twice and crits if either roll succeeds. This effectively increases your crit chance based on the formula: 1 - (1 - baseCrit)¬≤. For example, 50% base crit becomes 75% effective (1 - 0.5¬≤), while 10% becomes 19% (1 - 0.9¬≤). Duration: 630 seconds Stackable: Yes

üé≤ Experimental Mixture

A mysterious concoction crafted by Jens with unpredictable effects. Each potion grants one of 8 random buffs or debuffs. All effects have an equal 12.5% chance of occurring.

Experimental Mixture

This chaotic mixture can either greatly enhance your abilities or hinder your performance. Choose your moment wisely!

Effect: Random (1 of 8) Duration: ~3 mins or ~20 mins Stackable: No
Not Stackable: Experimental mixtures cannot stack with themselves. Drinking another while one is active is blocked to prevent illness!

‚≠ê Premium Buffs

These powerful consumables provide significant boosts to progression. They can be obtained from VIP chests, events, or the shop.

Experience Scroll (XP Scroll)

Reading this ancient scroll grants enhanced learning, boosting both level and rank experience gains. Perfect for accelerating your progression!

Effect: +50% XP Gain (1.5x) Duration: 30 minutes (1800s) Stackable: Yes (Refreshes)

Drop Booster (Loot Buff)

Using this booster increases the drop rate from all monsters, helping you find better loot and rare items more quickly.

Effect: +50% Drop Rate (1.5x) Duration: 30 minutes (1800s) Stackable: Yes (Refreshes)
Timer Refresh: Using another scroll or booster while one is active will add 30 minutes to the remaining duration, not replace it!
