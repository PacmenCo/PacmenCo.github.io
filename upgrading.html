⬆️ Item Upgrading

Permanently increase your item's base stats

What is Item Upgrading?

Item Upgrading is a system that allows you to permanently increase an item's base stats using Pixels (coins) and Crystals (enchanting materials). Each upgrade level increases the item's damage or armor by 5%, making it significantly more powerful.

How Upgrading Works

Each item has an itemStrength value that represents its upgrade level. When you upgrade an item:

  • The item's upgrade level (itemStrength) increases by 1
  • Damage or armor increases by 5% of the base value
  • Enchantments are preserved during upgrades
  • Cost increases exponentially with each level
Guaranteed Success: Item upgrades have a 100% success rate. Your enchantments and item properties are always preserved!

Stat Scaling Formula

Final Stat = Base Stat × Item Quality × (1 + itemStrength × 0.05)

Example:
Base Damage: 100
Item Quality: 1.5 (Epic)
Upgrade Level: 4
Final Damage = 100 × 1.5 × (1 + 4 × 0.05) = 100 × 1.5 × 1.2 = 180

Each upgrade level adds +5% multiplicative bonus. At upgrade level 10, your item deals +50% more damage/armor!

Item Upgrade Costs

Upgrade costs scale exponentially with upgrade level, item quality, and item rarity. Higher quality and rarer items cost significantly more to upgrade.

Pixel Cost Formula

Cost = itemTypeMultiplier × itemCost × 0.34 ×
1.9^((itemQuality/1.25 + 0.1)^1.6 + itemStrength/1.75) × (2 × itemQuality - 1.97)

The formula uses several constants to create exponential growth:

  • a = 0.34 (base cost multiplier)
  • b = 1.9 (exponential base)
  • c = 1.6 (quality exponent)
  • d = -0.1 (quality offset)
  • e = 1.75 (strength divisor)
  • f = 1.25 (quality divisor)

Crystal Cost Formula

ilvlScale = (itemLevel + 10) / 50 × 5

Cost = ilvlScale × itemTypeMultiplier ×
((rarity - 1)^2 + 0.4 × itemStrength^1.55 × (2 × rarity - 3))^1.35

Crystal costs increase based on:

  • Item level (higher level = more crystals)
  • Item rarity (exponential scaling)
  • Current upgrade level (power of 1.55)
  • Item type (weapon vs armor multipliers)

Max Upgrade Level

The maximum upgrade level you can achieve increases with your player level and ascension bonuses.

Max Upgrade Level = 1 + AscensionEnchantingModifier + playerLevel / (15 × levelsPerRealLevel)

This means:

  • Base max level: 1
  • Ascension skills can increase the cap (+1 from Enchanting skill)
  • Every 75 actual levels grants +1 max upgrade level
  • Post-prestige players use max level (301) instead of actual level
