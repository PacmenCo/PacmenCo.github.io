What is Idle Adventure?

Idle Adventure is a pixel style idle RPG where you battle fierce enemies with your team of Companions and Pets! Customize your character through your own Talent tree and Skill combination choices, and Craft increasingly powerful equipment. Join a Guild to defeat epic bosses, or embark on a solo adventure!
