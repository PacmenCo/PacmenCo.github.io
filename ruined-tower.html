Overview

The Ruined Tower is an endless wave survival challenge where you face increasingly powerful enemies. The raid itself is located in Ruined Tower - Zone 1. Entry requires a Tower Key. The Tower is the best location for Soulstone farming, with Evil Eyes (every 6th kill) dropping Soulstones at a 3% rate. The Ruined Tower can be a decent place to farm experience and loot as well.

  • Location: Ruined Tower - Zone 1
  • Entry Requirement: 1 Tower Key per run
  • Best Soulstone Farm: Evil Eyes (every 6th kill) offers a 3% drop rate
  • Endless Survival: Fight until you can no longer survive (but beware not to die and lose rank experience)
  • Fast Spawning: New enemies appear almost instantly and each kill extends the timer by 5 seconds (max 60s)

Scaling Analysis

  • Attack: Scales fastest at 2.04% per kill - damage becomes lethal quickly
  • Health: 1.45% per kill - enemies become increasingly tanky
  • Defense: 0.41% per kill - slowest scaling, less impactful
  • Level: Linear +0.1 per kill affects accuracy/evasion calculations

Survival Tip: Attack scaling is the deadliest factor. Prioritize defensive stats and damage mitigation to survive longer runs.

  • Experience: Scales with enemy level and number of kills

Soulstone Farming: With 3% drop rate from Evil Eyes (every 6th kill), expect roughly 1 Soulstone per ~200 total kills on average.

Strategy Guide

Build Requirements

  • Sustain Damage: Focus on consistent DPS rather than burst
  • Survivability: High HP, defense, and healing to outlast scaling damage
  • Loot modifier: Make sure your loot modifiers are high - maximize how many potions drop during the run!

Combat Strategy

  • Aggressive Killing: Don't let timer run low - kill constantly
  • Save Cooldowns for Evil Eyes: Every 6th kill is tougher, be ready
  • Heal Efficiently: Don't waste healing when unnecessary

Tower Key Crafting

Tower Keys are crafted using pixels and voxels. Like Cave Keys, they use a penalty system that increases cost but decays over time.

Penalty System

  • Penalty per purchase: 100 penalty per key crafted
  • Result: Faster decay than Cave Keys - costs normalize quicker

Decay per tick: 1 + penalty/80
Applied every 30 seconds

Decay Examples

  • At 80 penalty: Decay = 1 + (80/80) = 2 per tick
  • At 160 penalty: Decay = 1 + (160/80) = 3 per tick
  • At 400 penalty: Decay = 1 + (400/80) = 6 per tick

Conclusion: Higher penalties decay significantly faster. Tower Keys decay faster than Cave Keys (divider of 80 vs 115), making it easier to return to free key costs.
