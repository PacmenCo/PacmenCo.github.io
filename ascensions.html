What is Ascension?

Ascension is a prestige system that allows you to reset your character's level and progress in exchange for powerful permanent bonuses. Normal ascensions also grant Dream Points which unlock a special skill tree. Each ascension makes you stronger and unlocks new progression systems.

Ascension Requirements

  • Level Requirement: Must be level 301 or higher
  • No Locked Items: Must have no items in ascension-locked inventory
  • No Locked Currencies: All locked pixels, voxels, and crystals must be redeemed

Ascension Types

There are three types of ascensions, each with different difficulty levels and rewards.

Normal Ascension (Easy)

Unlock Requirement: Always available

Reward: 1 Dream Point per completion (unlimited)

What Happens:

  • Resets your level to 1 and XP to 0
  • All areas reset and must be unlocked again
  • You KEEP: All items, currencies, pets, talents, guild progress
  • First completion unlocks the Dream Point system

Hard Ascension (Medium)

Unlock Requirement: Must have defeated the "Bossy Roleplayer" boss

Reward: Permanent stat bonuses only (max 3 completions count)

Note: Additional rewards may be added in future updates

What Happens:

  • Resets your level to 1 and XP to 0
  • All areas reset
  • LOCKS AWAY: All equipped items, inventory, transmog, pixels, voxels, crystals
  • Progress bar multiplier resets from 1.01 â†’ 0.30
  • Auto-looting chance resets to 1
  • Offline rewards disabled
  • VIP chests unavailable
  • Items and currencies restored upon completion

Hardcore Ascension (Extreme)

Unlock Requirement: Must have completed at least 1 Hard Ascension

Reward: Permanent stat bonuses only (max 1 completion counts)

Note: Additional rewards may be added in future updates

What Happens:

  • Same as Hard Ascension
  • PERMADEATH: If you die, the ascension fails and converts to Normal
  • Most challenging ascension type
  • Higher enemy difficulty than Hard mode
Warning: In Hardcore mode, dying will fail your ascension! You lose the permanent stat bonus reward and your ascension converts to Normal mode. All locked items are restored, but you lose the prestige of completing Hardcore.

Permanent Ascension Bonuses

Each ascension type provides passive bonuses that are always active, even after completion. These bonuses have a maximum number of times they can be earned. These are SEPARATE from Dream Points - Normal ascensions give both bonuses AND Dream Points, while Hard/Hardcore only give bonuses.

Normal Ascension Bonuses (Max 5)

  • +15 base HP per completion
  • +1% damage per completion
  • +3% XP gain (level & rank) per completion
  • +3% loot drop chance per completion

Hard Ascension Bonuses (Max 3)

  • +20 base HP per completion
  • +1.5% damage per completion
  • +4% XP gain (level & rank) per completion
  • +3% loot drop chance per completion

Hardcore Ascension Bonuses (Max 1)

  • +50 base HP
  • +5% damage
  • +5% XP gain (level & rank)
  • +5% loot drop chance

Dream Points & Ascension Skills

Dream Points are special currency earned from completing Normal Ascensions. They're spent on powerful permanent upgrades called Ascension Skills.

Earning Dream Points

  • Normal Ascension: 1 Dream Point per completion (unlimited)
  • Hard Ascension: NO Dream Points (only permanent stat bonuses)
  • Hardcore Ascension: NO Dream Points (only permanent stat bonuses)
Important: Only Normal Ascensions grant Dream Points for the ascension skill tree! Hard and Hardcore ascensions provide powerful permanent stat bonuses instead, but do not give Dream Points to spend in the shop.
Strategy Tip: If you need more Dream Points for ascension skills, complete Normal ascensions. If you want stronger permanent bonuses, tackle Hard and Hardcore ascensions (which are much more challenging).

Ascension Skills (Dream Point Shop)

Ascension Skills are permanent upgrades purchased with Dream Points. Once bought, they're active forever and cannot be refunded.

Blacksmithing Skills

  • BlacksmithingLegend: +1.6% legendary craft chance per level (max 3) - Cost: 3 DP
  • BlacksmithingQuality: +4.5% item quality per level (max 2) - Cost: 2 DP
  • Enchanting: +1 max upgrade level (max 1) - Cost: 1 DP

Talent Skills

  • MoreSkill: +3 talent points per level (max 3, total +9) - Cost: 3 DP
  • SkillQuality: +3% talent effectiveness per level (max 5, total +15%) - Cost: 5 DP

Pet Skills

  • StrongPets: +1 max pet level per level (max 2) - Cost: 2 DP
  • BeastMaster: +15% active pet bonus per level (max 4, total +60%) - Cost: 4 DP

Combat Skills

  • IdleMaster: +5% idle/autoattack damage per level (max 3) - Cost: 3 DP
  • BossEater: +4.5% boss damage per level (max 4) - Cost: 4 DP
  • CounterAttack: +4% damage reflection per level (max 4) - Cost: 4 DP

Progression Skills

  • QuickLearner: +5% level XP per level (max 3) - Cost: 3 DP
  • BattleBrainy: +10% rank XP per level (max 5) - Cost: 5 DP
  • LuckSeeker: +1 progress bar level per level (max 3) - Cost: 3 DP
  • Greed: +5% drop chance per level (max 5) - Cost: 5 DP

Defense & Economy Skills

  • Defender: +4% armor per level (max 3) - Cost: 3 DP
  • Bank: +10% bank efficiency per level (max 2) - Cost: 2 DP

Unlimited Skill

No Refunds: Dream Points cannot be refunded once spent. Choose your ascension skills carefully!

Hardcore Mode Rules

Hardcore mode is the ultimate test of skill and patience, featuring permadeath and the harshest difficulty modifiers.

Special Rules

  • Permadeath: One death and you fail the ascension
  • Enemy HP: 185% of normal (highest of all modes)
  • Enemy Damage: 320% of normal (highest of all modes)
  • Extreme HP Penalty: Your HP from rank is drastically reduced early game
  • No Offline Rewards: Must play actively
  • No VIP Chests: Premium shortcuts disabled
  • All Items Locked: Start completely naked with no equipment

Failing Hardcore

When you die during a Hardcore Ascension:

  • Ascension immediately converts to Normal Ascension
  • All locked items and currencies are restored
  • You do NOT receive the permanent stat bonus reward
  • Progress toward Hardcore completion is lost
Strategy Tip: Hardcore is extremely challenging in the early levels (1-50) due to the HP penalty. Consider grinding levels carefully and avoiding risky situations until you've regained enough HP from leveling.

Resigning from Ascension

You can give up a Hard or Hardcore Ascension at any time.

What Happens When You Resign

  • Converts to Normal Ascension immediately
  • All locked items and currencies are restored
  • Offline rewards and VIP chests re-enabled
  • You do NOT receive the permanent stat bonus reward

Resigning is useful if you started a Hard/Hardcore run but realize you're not ready for the challenge. You keep your progress but lose the prestigious rewards.
