Squirrel Defense!

A powerful defensive ability that reduces incoming damage while simultaneously boosting Acornelius' attack power. Perfect for surviving dangerous encounters while maintaining offensive pressure.

  • Damage Reduction: 50% less damage taken (fixed)
  • Damage Boost: +65-95%+ damage for next 2-4 attacks (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Duration: 4-11 seconds (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Cooldown: 32 seconds
  • Stamina Cost: 4 stamina to activate
  • Range Bonus: +3m range (+0.6m effective)

Squirrel Defense provides both defensive and offensive benefits simultaneously, making Acornelius unique among defensive companions:

‚ö° Activation Prerequisites

To use Squirrel Defense effectively:

  • Manual Activation Required: Must press the Squirrel Defense button
  • Sufficient Stamina: Must have at least 4 stamina
  • Companion Awake: Companion must not be sleeping
  • Not on Cooldown: 32-second cooldown must be complete

Buff Tracking

The ability tracks two separate timers: the duration of the defensive buff (time-based) and the number of remaining boosted attacks (attack-based). You can monitor both to optimize your usage.

Pro Tip: The 32-second cooldown is relatively long, so timing your activation is crucial. Activate before or during dangerous encounters, not after taking heavy damage. The defensive buff gives you breathing room to heal or reposition while dealing increased damage.

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Strategy

When to Use Squirrel Defense

Activate Squirrel Defense when:

  • Facing tough enemies or bosses that deal high damage
  • Your HP is around 70-80% (before it gets critical)
  • Starting a difficult encounter where you know damage is coming
  • You have 4+ stamina and won't need it for other abilities soon
  • The 32-second cooldown is ready for the next dangerous phase

Buff Window Optimization

The key to Acornelius is maximizing damage during the boosted attack window:

  • Attack immediately after activation to use boosted attacks while buff is active
  • With high DEX, use all 4 boosted attacks in 2-3 seconds
  • The defensive buff continues even after boosted attacks are used
  • Extended range (+0.6m) allows safer positioning during buff

Cooldown Management

With a 32-second cooldown, Squirrel Defense is available roughly every 30-40 seconds of combat. This means you should use it liberally‚Äîdon't save it for "perfect" moments. If you're taking damage or facing a tough enemy, activate it. The defensive buff alone (50% reduction) is worth the cooldown.

Stamina Planning

Squirrel Defense costs 4 stamina, which is the highest cost among companions. With base 6 stamina, you can activate once and perform 2 attacks before needing to rest. Plan your stamina usage:

  • Ensure you have 4+ stamina before activating
  • After activation, you have enough stamina for 2 attacks (out of 2-4 boosted)
  • Higher CON/SPECIAL increases max stamina, allowing full buff usage
  • Consider resting before dangerous fights to enter with full stamina

Defensive Positioning

The 50% damage reduction doesn't mean you're invincible. Use the defensive window to:

  • Tank through damage spikes you'd normally avoid
  • Stay in melee range to maximize boosted attack usage
  • Use extended range (+0.6m) to maintain optimal distance
  • Heal during the defensive phase if HP is low
