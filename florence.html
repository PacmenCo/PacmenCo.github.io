Grasping Roots!

A powerful crowd control ability that completely immobilizes enemies while dealing massive damage. Florence channels nature's power to root enemies in place, neutralizing threats while dealing devastating attacks.

  • Damage Boost: +175-269%+ damage (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Root Duration: 1-5 seconds (scales with SPECIAL)
  • Stamina Cost: +2 stamina per attack while active, +3 stamina to apply root
  • Cooldown: 18 seconds (for root application)
  • Always Active: Damage boost applies continuously, root on cooldown

Root Mechanics

Grasping Roots is unique among companion abilities - the damage boost is always active when enabled, but rooting enemies has a cooldown. This creates a high-risk, high-reward playstyle:

Key Mechanic: When Grasping Roots is enabled, every attack costs +2 stamina (3 total). When you hit an enemy and the root cooldown is ready, an additional +3 stamina is consumed to apply the root (6 stamina total for that hit). This high stamina cost means you must manage resources carefully.

‚ö° Activation & Stamina Management

Activation Prerequisites

  • Manual Activation Required: Must press the Grasping Roots button
  • High Stamina Required: 3 stamina per normal attack, 6 stamina for root application
  • Companion Awake: Companion must not be sleeping
  • Root Cooldown: 18-second cooldown for root application (damage boost always active)

Stamina Cost Breakdown

Critical Limitation: Grasping Roots has the highest stamina cost of any companion ability. Every attack costs 3 stamina (base 1 + ability 2), and rooting an enemy costs an additional 3 stamina for a total of 6 stamina. With Florence's base max stamina of 5, you cannot apply a root without first increasing your max stamina through SPECIAL or CON investment.

Understanding stamina costs:

  • Normal attack without ability: 1 stamina
  • Attack with Grasping Roots active: 3 stamina (1 base + 2 ability)
  • Root application (when cooldown ready): 6 stamina total (3 for attack + 3 for root)
  • Base max stamina: 5 (3 + 0 SPECIAL/15 + 2 CON/5)

To use Grasping Roots effectively, you must invest in either:

  • CON to increase max stamina (every 5 CON = +1 stamina)
  • SPECIAL to increase max stamina (every 15 SPECIAL = +1 stamina)
  • Both for maximum stamina capacity

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Strategy

Managing Stamina Costs

The most important aspect of playing Florence is stamina management:

  • Always ensure you have 6+ stamina before attempting to root
  • Normal attacks (3 stamina) let you deal boosted damage while conserving stamina
  • Apply roots when the 18s cooldown is ready and you have sufficient stamina
  • Rest before dangerous fights to enter with full stamina
  • Higher CON/SPECIAL = more attacks per rest cycle

Root Timing

Save roots for optimal moments:

  • Priority targets: Fast enemies, hard-hitting enemies, bosses
  • Root before the enemy's next attack to prevent incoming damage
  • Use root windows to land multiple boosted attacks safely
  • 18-second cooldown allows frequent control (every ~20 seconds of combat)

Damage Optimization

Florence's +175-269% damage boost is always active while Grasping Roots is enabled. This means:

  • Every attack deals 2.7-3.7√ó damage continuously
  • Critical hits multiply this further (base 2√ó crit damage)
  • Rooted enemies cannot dodge or move, ensuring all attacks land
  • High damage per hit compensates for low attack speed

Risk vs. Reward

Grasping Roots is high-risk, high-reward:

  • Risk: Highest stamina cost means less uptime, more resting
  • Reward: Nearly 3√ó damage boost and complete enemy neutralization
  • Best against single powerful enemies where control matters
  • Less effective against swarms where stamina runs out quickly

When to Disable

Consider disabling Grasping Roots when:

  • Facing weak enemies that die in 1-2 hits (stamina cost not worth it)
  • Your stamina is too low to root (less than 6 max stamina)
  • You need more consistent uptime and less resting
