⚡ Skill System

Master powerful skills and devastating combos

Active Skills

Unlock and upgrade a diverse arsenal of active skills. Each skill has unique effects, cooldowns, and strategic uses. Combine different skills to create powerful synergies and overcome challenging encounters.

  • Offensive, defensive, and support skills
  • Elemental damage types and effects
  • Skill cooldown management
  • Skill upgrade and evolution system

Passive Abilities

Invest in passive abilities that provide permanent bonuses to your character. Build your character around different playstyles by choosing which passives to prioritize and upgrade.

  • Damage amplification passives
  • Defensive and survivability bonuses
  • Resource generation improvements
  • Special trigger effects

Skill Combos

Chain skills together to trigger powerful combo effects. Mastering the timing and order of your skills can dramatically increase your combat effectiveness and unlock bonus damage multipliers.
