Overview

Skills are combat abilities that you use in battle to defeat enemies. Each skill has unique properties like damage modifiers, cooldowns, and mana costs. You start with basic attacks and unlock more powerful abilities as you progress through the game.

Starting Skills: Quick Attack and Auto Attack
Skill Loadout: You can bring 4 skills of your choice to combat. In addition to these, you always have access to the Heal spell, giving you a total of 5 abilities in battle.
