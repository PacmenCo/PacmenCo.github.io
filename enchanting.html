Equipment Enhancement Systems Overview

Idle Adventure features three distinct equipment enhancement systems that work together to create powerful gear. Each system serves a unique purpose in optimizing your equipment for endgame progression. Understanding when and how to use each system is crucial for maximizing your character's power.

All three systems can be combined on the same item, allowing you to create exceptionally powerful equipment by upgrading stats, adding magical enchantments, and using Alteration Orbs to perfect item properties.
