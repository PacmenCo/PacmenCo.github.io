Overview

The Cosy Cave is a boss rush challenge located in The Pond - Zone 2. Entry requires a Cave Key. Face waves of increasingly powerful bosses in a limited time window, testing your build's strength against back-to-back encounters for massive XP and loot rewards.

  • Location: The Pond - Zone 2
  • Entry Requirement: 1 Cave Key per run
  • Massive XP: Each boss defeated grants significant experience points
  • More Rewards: Enemies in the cave have increased loot modifiers
  • Highscores and pets: High scores unlock special pets!

Scoring System

Your score determines rewards and is based on kills, remaining time, and difficulty multiplier.

Score = (kills + 1)^1.4 √ó ((timer + 30) / 3) √ó difficulty

Score Components

  • Kills: Number of bosses defeated (exponential scaling with 1.4 power)
  • Time Bonus: Extra 30 seconds added to remaining timer, divided by 3
  • Difficulty: Easy (1.0x), Normal (1.4x), Hard (2.2x)

Note: The exponential kills scaling means each additional boss defeated provides increasingly more score.

Strategy Guide

Preparation

  • Choose Difficulty Wisely: Match difficulty to your gear and level to optimize your rewards per key
  • Balance your Build: The Cosy Cave favors builds that can do both high damage and survivability

Cave Key Crafting

Cave Keys are crafted using Pixels and Voxels. The cost increases with each key crafted but also decays over time.

Penalty System

  • Penalty per purchase: 130 penalty per key crafted
  • Result: Costs gradually decrease if you stop crafting keys (faster decay at higher penalties)

Decay per tick: 1 + penalty/115
Applied every 30 seconds

Decay Examples

  • At 115 penalty: Decay = 1 + (115/115) = 2 per tick
  • At 230 penalty: Decay = 1 + (230/115) = 3 per tick
  • At 575 penalty: Decay = 1 + (575/115) = 6 per tick

Conclusion: Higher penalties decay significantly faster ‚Äîthe more you've crafted recently, the faster your penalty decreases.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get Cave Keys for The Cosy Cave?
Cave Keys are crafted using Pixels and Voxels. Your first key is free (0-9 penalty range). Each key crafted adds 130 penalty, increasing costs. Penalties decay over time at a rate of 1 + (penalty/115) every 30 seconds. Check the shop regularly for free keys. You can hold up to 3 keys maximum.
What difficulty should I choose for The Cosy Cave?
Choose the hardest difficulty where you can defeat all bosses. Hard difficulty provides a 2.2x score multiplier, Normal gives 1.4x, and Easy gives 1.0x. Match difficulty to your current gear and level - it's better to complete on a lower difficulty than fail partway through a harder one.
How is score calculated in The Cosy Cave?
Score formula: (kills + 1)^1.4 √ó ((timer + 30) / 3) √ó difficulty. Kills use exponential scaling (1.4 power), meaning each additional boss provides increasingly more score. Remaining time gets +30 bonus seconds added then divided by 3. Difficulty multiplier is Easy (1.0x), Normal (1.4x), or Hard (2.2x).
What rewards do I get from The Cosy Cave?
Each boss defeated grants significant experience points. Enemies in the cave have increased loot modifiers for better drops. High scores unlock special pets. The sequential boss rush format makes it an excellent XP farming location when you have Cave Keys available.
What level should I be for The Cosy Cave?
Level 301 is optimal. At this level, you have the best hit chance against bosses and gain a 5% damage bonus. However, you can attempt The Cosy Cave at lower levels by choosing an appropriate difficulty. Start with Easy difficulty if under-leveled, then progress to Normal and Hard as your build improves.
