‚ö° Activation Prerequisites

To use Runic Mark effectively, understand these activation conditions:

  • Manual Activation Required: Must press the Runic Mark button
  • Sufficient Stamina: Must have at least 4 stamina available
  • Companion Awake: Companion must not be sleeping
  • Not on Cooldown: 40-second cooldown must be complete
  • Enemy Must Be Hit: Mark only applies on successful hit after activation

Smart Auto-Behavior

Once activated, Hootini intelligently manages the mark:

  • If enemy is unmarked: Next hit applies the mark (costs 3 stamina)
  • If enemy is marked: Automatically enables +25-81% damage bonus (no stamina cost)
  • Won't waste marks on already-marked enemies
  • Cooldown starts AFTER mark is applied, not when button is pressed
Pro Tip: The mark lasts 12 seconds but the cooldown is 40 seconds. This means there's a 28-second gap where no marks are active. Plan your mark timing for tough enemies or boss encounters to maximize efficiency.

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Strategy

Mark Timing

Save Runic Mark for:

  • Elite enemies with high HP
  • Boss encounters where the 12-second window matters most
  • Situations where the shared debuff helps other companions/player
  • When you have 4+ stamina available and won't need it soon

Maximizing Mark Uptime

With a 40-second cooldown and 12-second duration, marks have 30% uptime. Use this window efficiently:

  • Attack as fast as possible during the mark window (DEX helps)
  • Save high-damage player abilities for marked enemies
  • Don't waste marks on enemies that will die quickly
  • Plan mark timing around boss mechanics or damage phases

Stamina Management

Marking costs 3 stamina. With 6 base stamina, you can mark twice before resting. Higher CON and SPECIAL increase max stamina, allowing more frequent marks. Balance mark usage with companion uptime‚Äîrunning out of stamina means no attacks while sleeping.

Team Synergy

The shared debuff makes Hootini excellent for team-based gameplay. The 3-27% damage amplification applies to all damage sources, including:

  • Your player character's attacks
  • Other companions' damage
  • Damage-over-time effects (burns, etc.)
  • Critical hits and ability damage

Against Unmarked Enemies

Without marks active, Hootini's high LUCK (15) makes it effective through critical hits. The low damage scaling means you won't deal exceptional damage, but consistent crits keep DPS respectable between mark windows.
